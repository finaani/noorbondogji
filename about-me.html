My Story

I'm Noor Bondogji, and this is my porfolio for Digital Media Art. I am a Junior at San Jose State University studying Digital Media.I love making digital art because it is so fascinating to produce work on computer and bring it to life.

I grew up in the Bay Area and is currently a student but in the future I aspire to work in a company as a digital artist making designs and marketing advertisments. My many skills include, Adobe Suite (photoshop, illustration, premier pro), Photography/Videography, and Web Develeopment.

My work is described more colorful and vibrant. I like to work with colors and emotions and focusing more on illustrative designs, but lately I have been more into digital art using animations. I am still learning and always will. I like to put my skill to test and hope you like them too.
