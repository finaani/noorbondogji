Interupted Peace

This is glitch art. The idea for this project was to take something peaceful and distort it. When we go about our days, we usually are stressed easily because of the amount of work we do daily. The medium used for this piece is Photoshop, Illustrator, and TextEditor. To distort the image and make it glitchy, I used text editor where I deleted, added, manipulated the images texts to get the glitch effect.
