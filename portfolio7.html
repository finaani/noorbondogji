Mind Clutter

This video is about going back to our memories and thinking about the past. Sometimes we hold on to our pasts and it clutters our minds leaving the important things on hold. The medium I used was a Nikon D3100 and Premiere for editing the video. I used After Effects to do the rewind of the video and audio. I also altered the audio to a more high pitched voice to symbol irritation. There are glitches added to give it a old memory vibe.
